## Description (English)

**SmartCard helps you choose the right credit card for every purchase to maximize your rewards.**

Stop leaving money on the table! SmartCard analyzes your credit cards and instantly tells you which card to use for any purchase category or merchant.

**KEY FEATURES:**

**Smart Card Recommendations**
- Get instant recommendations based on spending category or merchant
- See potential rewards before you swipe
- Compare all your cards side-by-side

**Rotating Category Tracking**
- Never miss quarterly bonus categories (Chase Freedom Flex, Discover it, etc.)
- Get reminders when new quarters begin
- Track your progress toward spending caps

**Selectable Category Optimization**
- Manage cards with user-selectable bonus categories
- Get recommendations for optimal category selections based on your spending habits

**Spending Tracking**
- Log transactions manually or scan receipts with OCR
- Track rewards earned across all your cards
- See detailed analytics and charts
- Identify missed reward opportunities

**Spending Cap Alerts**
- Monitor progress toward quarterly/annual caps
- Get alerts before you hit your limits
- Maximize bonus rewards without overspending

**SUPPORTED CARDS:**
- Chase Sapphire Preferred/Reserve
- Chase Freedom Flex/Unlimited
- American Express Gold/Platinum
- Citi Double Cash/Custom Cash
- Capital One Venture/SavorOne
- Discover it Cash Back
- Bank of America Customized Cash
- Wells Fargo Active Cash
- US Bank Cash+
- And many more!

**WHY SMARTCARD?**
Credit card rewards can be confusing with rotating categories, spending caps, and different multipliers. SmartCard cuts through the complexity and gives you simple, actionable recommendations.

Download SmartCard today and start earning more rewards on every purchase!

## App Review Notes

SmartCard is a personal finance tool that helps users optimize their credit card rewards. The app:

1. Does NOT process actual financial transactions
2. Does NOT store credit card numbers, CVVs, or sensitive financial data
3. Only stores card names and reward category preferences
4. Uses Apple's Vision framework for receipt OCR (on-device processing)
5. Requires Sign in with Apple for account creation (if Firebase auth is enabled)
